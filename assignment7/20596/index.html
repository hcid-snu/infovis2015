Date Place Map

This choropleth encodes frequency of date places within Seoul based on data from BetweenDate(http://betweendate.com).
Mapogu(1118), Junggu(1046), and Gangnamgu(1025) are shown to be most popular among Seoul couples.
Red dots indicate the most reviewed date place for food in the Gu-area, and
blue dots indicate the most reviewed date place for other couple activities(e.g. walking, sightseeing, picnic-ing) in the Gu-area.
